About this provider

At WCG, we offer a bespoke solution that makes it easy to take on and up-skill apprentices. We handle all administration and training arrangements, as well as mentoring apprentices so you get the best results for your business and your apprentices receive the best start to their career.

WCG trains over 2000 apprentices every year and offers world-class facilities, expert tuition and dedicated support. We have an excellent track record of high-quality training and work with over 960 employers, from large global organisations to small local businesses.

With over 70 frameworks plus more being developed all the time, we can help you to develop an apprenticeship programme that meets your training needs and leads to your business growth.
